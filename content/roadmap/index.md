### Current Phase: <a class="red"> Intial Sign Ups </a>

### Initial Testing Phase

This phase featured experimentation of Wireless systems and end to end integration testing

### Intial Sign Ups

Getting Initial Sign Ups and Funding mechanisms. This phase features getting people centered funding and no corporate or government backing to insure that the internet is communuity and people owned first are foremost.

### Funding Phase

Determine if there will be business and government grant funding pursuits.

### Initial Installs

Deployments of initial wireless backhaul to give accessible internet to a single building with a good geographic position.
Deploy client internet services for people who signed up in the initial phase.

### Documentation and Streamlined Sign Ups

Revamp documentation to account for changes during set up. Streamline sign up process to be more optimized and informative for users who sign up for future service.

### Recalibrations

Rethink project to make sure things are moving in the correct and sustainable direction.

### Growth

Expand Wireless Mesh to all of DC that wants it.

### Continued Community Engagements

Form partnerships with local organizations to ensure we are delivering what the people want.
